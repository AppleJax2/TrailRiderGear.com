## What to Avoid

Certain approaches consistently appear in user reports as problematic:

**Magnetic mounts** generate multiple reports of rod drops on rough terrain. When vibration exceeds the magnetic holding force, particularly on descents or washboard sections, rods simply fall out.

**Suction cup systems** receive near-universal negative feedback in outdoor forums. Temperature changes break seals and dust contamination renders them ineffective almost immediately upon leaving paved roads.

**DIY PVC solutions** remain functional for gentle conditions. But user reports of rod damage when homemade mounts fail on rough roads are common enough to warrant serious caution before trusting expensive gear to hardware store plumbing fittings.
